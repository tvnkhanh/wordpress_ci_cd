== Description ==
Introducing ShopGrove, the ultimate WordPress Full Site Editing (FSE) theme meticulously tailored for WooCommerce integration. With intuitive controls and unparalleled customization options, ShopGrove empowers you to effortlessly design every aspect of your online store without touching a single line of code. Elevate your eCommerce experience with responsive design, SEO-friendly structure, and dynamic templates crafted specifically for WooCommerce FSE. Whether you're launching a new store or revamping an existing one, ShopGrove ensures your online presence is visually captivating, engaging customers, and driving sales like never before.
